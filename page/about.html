Why It Matters

Every year, nearly one-third of all food produced globally is wasted. This causes major harm to our environment by increasing greenhouse gases and wasting resources like water, energy, and labor.

Understanding this problem helps us take steps to reduce waste, save money, and protect our planet.

How To Reduce

Start by buying only what you need, storing food properly, and using leftovers creatively. Label and date your food containers and understand expiry vs. best-before dates.

Small actions like these make a big difference!

Read More

Simple Actions

Use meal planners and shopping lists. Share extra food with neighbors or donate. Compost food scraps and get creative with peelings, stems, and leftovers.

Be a food-saving hero with easy daily habits!
